How It Works

This tool estimates your salary per second by calculating your total annual income divided by the number of seconds you work in a year. You can input your salary in different formats:

  • Annual: Your total yearly salary
  • Monthly: Your monthly salary (multiplied by 12)
  • Hourly: Your hourly rate (multiplied by total working hours)

For working hours, you can specify:

  • Weekly: Hours worked per week
  • Daily: Hours worked per day (assumes 5 working days)
  • Monthly: Hours worked per month (converted to weekly)

The calculation converts everything to annual income and weekly hours, then divides by the total working seconds in a year (hours √ó 52 weeks √ó 3600 seconds).

Frequently Asked Questions

No. All calculations happen locally in your browser, ensuring privacy.
Why does the live counter update every second?
To give you a dynamic and real-time visualization of your earnings as time passes.
Can I use this tool for hourly or monthly salary?
You can convert your salary to annual figures and input those values for accurate results.
